ATLAS Social Publisher

ATLAS Social Publisher is a creator-operated publishing tool that helps the account owner upload original, human-approved videos to their own TikTok and social media accounts. Content is never uploaded or published without the account owner's explicit authorization.

About This Application

ATLAS Social Publisher is a local, self-hosted tool designed for individual content creators. It integrates with TikTok via the official TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API to allow the account owner to schedule and publish their own original content.

Key Features

  • Upload videos to TikTok Inbox/Draft for review before publishing
  • Human approval required before any content is posted
  • Secure local storage of authorization tokens
  • No content is published without the account owner's explicit consent
  • Full control to revoke access and disconnect at any time
