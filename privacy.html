Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. About This Application

ATLAS Social Publisher is a local, creator-operated publishing tool. It is designed for use by the account owner only. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles data when integrating with TikTok via the official TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API.

2. Data We Access

When you authorize ATLAS Social Publisher to connect with your TikTok account, the application may access:

  • Your TikTok account identifier (open_id) to verify account identity
  • Basic account information (display name) for confirmation purposes
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to maintain the authorized connection
  • Granted permission scopes (e.g., video.upload)

3. How We Store Data

  • All tokens and credentials are stored locally on your own device, not on any external server
  • Tokens are stored securely and are never transmitted to third parties
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to maintain the TikTok integration
  • No data is stored in source code or version control systems

4. How We Use Data

The application uses your data solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Upload videos to your TikTok Inbox/Draft when you explicitly approve them
  • Verify your account identity before publishing
  • Refresh the access token to maintain an active connection

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party
  • We do not publish any content without your explicit approval
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data beyond what is necessary for operation
  • We do not store your TikTok password or login credentials
  • We do not upload content you do not own or have rights to

6. TikTok Integration

This application uses the official TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok through this integration is also subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and TikTok's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights and Controls

  • You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through the ATLAS Social Publisher settings
  • You can revoke access directly from your TikTok account settings at any time
  • You can request deletion of any locally stored data - see our Data Deletion Instructions

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
